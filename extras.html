Optional extras

A few smart add-ons to stop losing work.

You can't answer the phone with your head under a truck, or at 2am. The bloke whose truck is down won't leave a voicemail, he rings the next mechanic. These few add-ons catch that work for you, day and night. Here's what each one does and how it helps.

Missed-call text-back

The big one

Miss a call, and it texts them straight back for you.

  • Within seconds they get a message: "On a job right now. Send your truck, the fault and where you are, and I'll call you straight back."
  • Keeps the customer with you instead of ringing the next mechanic down the list.
  • The job details land on your phone while you're still under the truck.

Website enquiry helper

The big one

A smart chat on your website that asks the right questions for you.

  • Every enquiry lands with the vehicle, the fault, how urgent it is and where they are, already sorted.
  • No more vague "give us a call" messages with nothing to go on.
  • Tells a real breakdown apart from a routine service, so you know what to drop everything for.

After-hours auto-reply

Built in

Answers for you when you're asleep or flat out.

  • Anyone who messages after hours hears back instantly, so no one's left wondering if you got it.
  • Lets them know you run 24/7 for genuine breakdowns, and you'll sort scheduled work in the morning.
  • Comes built into the two above. Nothing extra to pay.

Automatic review requests

Do this next

Texts the customer for a Google review once the job's done.

  • More Google reviews means more trust, and you show up higher when someone searches for a mechanic.
  • One tap for the customer, nothing for you to chase up.
  • Best switched on once the lead-catchers above are running.
