06 Questions, sorted

The stuff fleet managers ask first.

Q-01 Do you come to me?

Yes. EMTR is fully mobile. We bring the workshop to your depot, work site or the roadside, anywhere across Sydney and NSW. No tow truck, no truck off the road for a week.

Q-02 What areas do you cover?

Sydney metro, Western Sydney and greater NSW, with a service radius of around 60 km from Western Sydney plus regional callouts. Not sure if we reach you? Give us a call and we will tell you straight.

Q-03 Are you available after hours and on weekends?

We run 24/7 for breakdowns, including weekends and public holidays. Scheduled work booked outside business hours carries no after-hours charge.

Q-04 Will servicing with you affect my manufacturer warranty?

No. We carry out logbook and scheduled servicing to manufacturer specifications and provide detailed service records after every job, so your warranty stays intact.

Q-05 What vehicles and brands do you work on?

Trucks, trailers, fleet vehicles, earthmoving and machinery. Engine families include Cummins, CAT, Detroit, PACCAR, Isuzu, Hino and Fuso, among others.

Q-06 How fast can you get to a breakdown?

Breakdowns jump the queue. Call us and we will give you an honest ETA on the spot, then get rolling.

Q-07 Do you offer fleet maintenance plans?

Yes. EMTR Fleet Care is a tailored preventative-maintenance program built around your run, with invoicing accounts available for business customers.
